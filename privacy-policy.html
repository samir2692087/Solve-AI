Privacy Policy

For the mobile application Solve AI
Last Updated: 25 November 2025

Solve AI (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a calculator and AI assistance application designed to help users perform mathematical calculations and access AI-generated answers. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Solve AI does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information from users.

a) Calculation Input

  • Numbers, expressions, and operations you enter into the calculator are processed locally on your device.
  • This information is not stored or transmitted to any server by us.

b) AI Query Input

  • When you use the AI feature, the text you type is sent to an AI service solely to generate a response.
  • We do not store, sell, or create profiles from these queries.

c) Non-Personal Technical Information

  • App version and basic device information (such as device model and operating system version).
  • Anonymous crash logs and performance data, used only to improve app stability and performance.

2. How We Use the Information

We may use non-personal and technical information to:

  • Provide and improve calculator functionality and AI responses.
  • Fix bugs and improve app performance and reliability.

We do not use your information for:

  • Advertising or ad targeting.
  • Selling data to third parties.
  • Creating personal profiles or tracking you across apps or websites.

3. Third-Party Services

Solve AI may use third-party services, such as AI or analytics providers, to:

  • Generate AI-based answers from the queries you submit.
  • Collect anonymous crash logs and performance metrics.

These services process your data only to the extent necessary to provide their functionality. We do not share any additional personal information with them because we do not collect such data.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • We do not maintain personal user databases on our own servers.
  • Calculator inputs are handled on your device and are not stored by us.
  • AI queries may be processed temporarily by the AI provider to return results, and are not used by us to identify you.

5. Children’s Privacy

Solve AI does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. As we do not collect personally identifiable information, the app is designed with user privacy in mind for all age groups. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will revise the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page. We recommend reviewing this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
