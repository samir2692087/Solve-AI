1. Introduction

Welcome to Solve AI (“we”, “our”, “us”). We value your privacy and are committed to keeping your data secure and transparent.

Solve AI is a smart calculator and AI assistance application that helps you perform mathematical calculations and get AI-generated explanations or answers.

By using the application, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

No sign-up & no personal account required
📋
2. Information Collection & Usage

Solve AI is designed to collect only the minimum information required to operate the app. We do not ask you to create an account and we do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, address, or phone number.

a) Calculator Inputs

  • Numbers, formulas, and expressions you type are processed locally on your device.
  • We do not store or transmit your calculator history to our own servers.

b) AI Query Inputs

  • When you ask a question in the AI section, the text you enter is sent to an AI service provider solely to generate a response for you.
  • We do not use these queries to build personal profiles, and we do not sell this data.

  • App version and basic device information (e.g., device model, OS version).
  • Anonymous crash logs or performance data to help improve stability and fix bugs.

All of this information is used only to provide and improve the app’s core features: calculations, AI answers, and app reliability.

🤖
3. AI Responses & Service Providers

To generate AI-based answers, Solve AI connects to a trusted artificial intelligence service. Your question or prompt is processed by that service to return a helpful response.

  • Your text is transmitted over an encrypted connection whenever possible.
  • The data is processed only for generating a reply and is not used by us for marketing or advertising.
  • We do not knowingly send any persistent identifiers (such as your name or email) because the app does not collect this information.

These third-party services may have their own privacy practices. We encourage you to review their policies if you want more detail on how they handle data on their side.

💾
4. Local Storage

In some cases, the app may store recent calculation history or simple preferences (such as last used mode) directly on your device.

  • This data stays on your device and is not automatically uploaded to our servers.
  • You can clear app data from your system settings if you wish to remove it.
🔒
5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information handled through the app.

  • Communication with AI services is made over secure connections where supported.
  • We collect only minimal, non-personal technical data needed for functionality.
  • We do not maintain large personal data stores on our own servers.
⚠
Important:

Please avoid entering highly sensitive information such as financial passwords, full payment card numbers, or private medical records into the AI input. While your data is processed securely, the app is intended for educational and general assistance purposes only.

📝
6. Children & Policy Updates

a) Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Because we do not require accounts or sign-ups, the app is designed to minimize personal data collection for users of all ages.

b) Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top. Continued use of the app after any change means you accept the updated policy.
